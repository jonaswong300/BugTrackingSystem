Step to reproduce:
1. Open attached
2. Middle mouse click on lightgray area to start autoscroll
3. move mouse pointer downward
4. When the autoscroll marker is positioned over the image, Move the mouse pointer over the marker.
5. Mouse left/middle click on the marker to leave autoscroll
